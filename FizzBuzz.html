Interactive FizzBuzz! Input an integer, press "FizzBuzz!", and this will spit out an array containing all the numbers from 1 to the input integer, with the appropriate numbers replaced with either 'Fizz,' 'Buzz,' or 'FizzBuzz.' (multiples of 3 should be 'Fizz,' multiple of 5 should be 'Buzz,' and multiples of both should be 'FizzBuzz')
